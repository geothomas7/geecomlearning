Questions

Things people ask before they subscribe

What counts as one request?

A request is one deliverable — a single module, a video script, a set of slides, a storyboard. Complex projects get broken into individual requests so we can move fast without losing quality.

How quickly will I get work back?

Most requests are turned around in 2–5 business days. Larger projects (full eLearning courses, multi-video series) take longer, but we'll always give you a clear timeline upfront.

What if I need more than one thing at a time?

We work through your queue in priority order — one focused deliverable at a time. If you have a high volume of urgent requests, we can discuss a higher-tier plan with parallel work streams.

Can I pause if my workload drops?

Yes, anytime. Billing pauses and resumes in full billing cycles. There's no minimum commitment and no cancellation fee.

Do I need to provide the content?

Nope. We can work from rough notes, existing materials, SME interviews, or just a brief description of what needs to be learned. The less polished the input, the more we can help shape it.

Is there a trial or intro offer?

We start with a free 30-minute intro call to understand your needs and make sure the subscription model is the right fit. From there, you can jump straight in — no pilot projects required.
