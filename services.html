What we do

Training content built around your exact need

We make bespoke eLearning and training content — commissioned project by project, built to fit what your team actually needs. We also offer an emerging subscription service for organisations that want ongoing L&D support.

Service 1 — Our core offering

Custom content creation

You tell us what you need. We scope it, price it, and build it. Every project is quoted individually based on the format, length, and complexity involved — no standard packages, because no two briefs are the same.

eLearning Courses

Interactive, SCORM/xAPI-ready modules built for real learners — not just checkbox compliance. We write the scenarios, design the interactions, and deliver files your LMS can actually use.

Best for: Onboarding, compliance, product knowledge, skills training

Onboarding & Training Videos

Role-specific videos that give people the clarity they need — without a six-hour orientation. We script, produce, and edit content that reflects your actual culture, not a corporate template.

Best for: New hire programs, member orientation, role-specific onboarding

Compliance & Safety Training

Documented, defensible, and built to hold up under scrutiny. We design compliance programs for industries where the stakes are real — regulated environments, high-risk workplaces, and audit-sensitive operations.

Best for: Regulated industries, safety-critical workplaces, documentation requirements

Microlearning

Short, punchy learning assets — 3 to 7 minutes, designed to be absorbed not endured. Great for policy updates, refreshers, and knowledge that needs to stick across a busy workforce.

Best for: Busy teams, recurring reinforcement, just-in-time knowledge

LMS / SCORM / xAPI Support

We package content to the spec your LMS requires and test it before delivery. We work with TalentLMS, Docebo, Moodle, and most other platforms.

Best for: Teams with existing LMS infrastructure, reporting requirements

How pricing works: Every project is scoped and quoted individually. We'll talk through your brief, give you a clear estimate, and only begin once you're happy. No surprises.

Service 2 — Ongoing L&D support

L&D as a subscription

For organisations with a consistent, ongoing training need, we offer a flat monthly retainer — your team's dedicated L&D resource, without the overhead of hiring in-house. Instead of quoting individual projects, you have a set amount of capacity available every month to work through your training backlog.

It's a straightforward arrangement: we stay close to your team, understand your training priorities, and keep the work moving. No re-briefing, no delays, no surprises on cost.

What it could include

Instructional design, video production, eLearning development, content writing, LMS support, and project management — all available as part of one monthly arrangement.

Who it might suit

Organisations with regular, ongoing training needs — say, monthly compliance updates, continuous onboarding, or an L&D team that needs consistent production support.

A consistent pipeline of work

The retainer works best when there's a regular flow of training needs — new policies, evolving products, team growth, seasonal compliance updates. We stay ready, so you don't have to re-brief us every time.

We're thinking in the region of $3,000/month, but the exact structure would depend on your volume and what you actually need. Worth a chat if you're curious.
