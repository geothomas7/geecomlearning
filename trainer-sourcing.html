Specialist Trainer Sourcing

We find, vet, and place qualified specialist trainers for corporate workshops and short engagements. You get the right person in the room. We handle the rest.

When the training you need is a person, not a course

Sometimes what your team needs is not a module we can build. It is a qualified instructor with real subject matter depth, someone who has done the work, not just studied it, standing in front of your people for a day.

Finding that person takes time. The trainers worth hiring are not listed on a freelancer marketplace. They work inside regulatory bodies, research institutions, professional associations, and specialist consultancies. Each one has a different way of being reached, a different way of quoting, and a different lead time.

You give us a brief. We come back with a shortlist of vetted candidates and a placement plan. If we cannot find someone who fits, there is no fee.

Best for

  • One-off workshops on specialist topics
  • Short engagements of 1–5 days
  • Topics where the trainer’s qualifications and practical experience matter
  • Teams without an internal Learning & Development function
  • Briefs you have already tried to fill yourself
How it works

From brief to trainer in the room

01

Brief

We spend 30–45 mins understanding what you need. Audience, topic, format, dates, budget, and what “qualified” looks like for this particular session.

02

Source

We reach out through the right channels for the brief. That might be a professional association, an institutional training office, a specialist consultancy, or someone in our own network. We do not rely on a single roster.

03

Vet

We verify qualifications, check recent delivery experience, and have a working call with each shortlisted candidate. You meet the final 2 or 3, not the long list.

04

Place

We handle the contract, the scheduling, pre-session coordination, and invoicing. The trainer arrives prepared. You stay focused on everything else.

What we source for

Topic areas

Examples of the briefs we take on. If your topic is not listed, ask.

Statistics, data literacy, and analytics

Trainers from applied research backgrounds, statistical agencies, and independent consultancies. Anything from foundational data literacy to advanced workshops on inference, survey design, or predictive modelling.

Regulatory and compliance specialists

Practitioners who have worked inside the regulatory environment that governs your industry. Not someone who has read the guidance, but someone who helped write it or enforce it.

Sector and operational training

Technical instructors with hands-on industry experience in manufacturing, energy, transportation, healthcare, or public sector operations. We screen for teaching capability alongside subject knowledge.

Leadership and people management

Facilitators with a track record of running corporate workshops on management fundamentals, performance conversations, and change leadership. We assess how they deliver, not just what is on their CV.

Technical upskilling and emerging topics

Cybersecurity, applied artificial intelligence (AI) literacy, environmental, social and governance (ESG) reporting, accessibility, and data protection. Specialists who actively work in the field and can teach from experience.

Custom briefs

Niche topics where we have to go and find the right person. A former head of a specific regulatory programme, a recognised academic in a narrow field, or an operator from a particular institution. If they exist and they take corporate work, we will find them.

Scope

Where this service fits

This service is for corporate, workforce, and worksite training only. The brief is always tied to a workplace skill, a regulatory requirement, or a capability the organisation needs to build. If your need sits outside that scope, we will say so at the briefing call and point you to a better channel where we can.

Not a fit for

  • Personal training, fitness, or wellness coaching for individuals
  • University executive education referrals, or sourcing instructors for university degree programmes and accredited certifications
  • Long-term staffing or permanent hiring of an in-house trainer
  • Self-serve marketplace browsing (we do not publish a public roster)
  • Train-the-trainer programmes for your existing internal trainers
How fees work

Quoted per engagement, contingent on placement

We quote a sourcing fee for each engagement. That is either a percentage of the trainer’s contracted fee or a flat fee for harder-to-find specialists. The trainer sets their own rate and we do not mark it up. Our fee is explained in writing before any work begins, and it is contingent on placement. If we cannot find a trainer who fits, you do not pay.

Why use us instead of searching yourself

We are a Learning & Development agency, not a recruitment firm. We evaluate how a trainer teaches, how they handle a room, and whether their qualifications translate into effective delivery. A strong researcher does not always make a strong workshop facilitator. We know how to tell the difference.

Our sourcing fee is quoted upfront in writing. No silent markups on the trainer’s rate. No obligation if we cannot find a fit.

Common questions

Trainer sourcing FAQs

How is this different from a marketplace like Catalant or Upwork?

We are not a self-serve platform. You give us a brief and we go find the right person, vet them, and place them. There is no roster to browse. The sourcing channels we use are not searchable in one place, which is the whole reason this service exists.

How long does sourcing take?

Common topics can usually be matched within 1–2 weeks. Niche briefs where we need a very specific profile can take 3–6 weeks. We will give you a realistic timeline at the briefing stage.

What happens if you cannot find a suitable trainer?

You pay nothing. If we cannot find someone who fits the brief, we say so and there is no charge. We would rather turn down a brief than place the wrong person.

Have a brief in mind?

Tell us the audience, the topic, the dates, and what a qualified trainer looks like. We will come back within 2 business days with whether we can source it and how long it would take.
