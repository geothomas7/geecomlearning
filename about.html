Our story

About Geecom Learning

Founded by people who've spent years inside workplace education, doing the work, not selling software.

The L&D team you don't have

You know your team needs better training. But you don't have a Learning and Development (L&D) department, and hiring one isn't in the budget. So it falls on Operations, or Human Resources, or whoever draws the short straw, and the project stalls.

Geecom Learning exists to be the L&D team you don't have. Whether you need a single bespoke course built from scratch or an ongoing training partner on a flat monthly fee, we bring the instructional design, production, and technical setup so you can get training out the door without building an internal department to do it.

Founded on experience, not software

We're founded by people who've spent years inside workplace education, building certification programs, writing training standards, and auditing providers. Not selling software. Actually doing the work.

Outcomes first, always

Everything we build is outcomes based. Every course, module, and video is designed so learners walk away with something measurable, whether that's a new competence they can apply on the job, a deeper awareness of risk and responsibility, or the foundational knowledge they need to perform safely and effectively. That's what shapes our process from day one: training that actually changes what people know and what they can do.
